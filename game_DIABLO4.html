Diablo 4

Diablo 4 is the highly anticipated fourth installment in the action role-playing game series developed by Blizzard Entertainment. The game is set in the dark and gothic world of Sanctuary, where players face the forces of Hell in a quest to save humanity. Diablo 4 introduces a new open-world setting with non-linear gameplay, allowing players to explore different regions and encounter a variety of enemies.
