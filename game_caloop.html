Get ready to exercise your brain cells with the upcoming puzzle game, "Caloop," a mathematical marvel set to challenge players in a unique and strategic way. Developed by Foolhardy Games, the masterminds behind the creation include Bradley Jones as the lead programmer, Ruben Gardiner as the lead artist, and Julio Reyes lending his expertise to the audio department. In "Caloop," players engage in a captivating puzzle-solving experience by placing segments featuring various operators and values onto a wheel to construct an equation. The real challenge lies in strategic placement, as once the wheel is filled, players give it a spin and eagerly await the outcome, hoping that their calculated moves align with the target number. With its innovative approach to combining mathematics and puzzle-solving, "Caloop" promises to be a brain-teasing adventure for gamers of all levels.
