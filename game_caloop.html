Caloop

Caloop is a mathematical puzzle game. You place segments containg different operators and values on a wheel to form an equation. Once your wheel is full, you give it a spin and hope the result of your stratetgic placement matches the target number.

Prepare for a mind-bending adventure as Foolhardy Games, the visionary developers who brought you the rhythm-infused "Gun Groove Alley," step into the realm of mathematics with their latest creation, "Caloop." The brainchild of Bradley Jones, the lead programmer, artistic brilliance by Ruben Gardiner, and the immersive soundscape crafted by Julio Reyes, "Caloop" introduces a novel approach to puzzle gaming. Players will strategically place segments featuring diverse operators and values onto a wheel to construct equations. Once the wheel is brimming with mathematical potential, a spin unveils the culmination of strategic choices, with players crossing their fingers for a match with the target number. With the same innovative spirit that characterized "Gun Groove Alley," Foolhardy Games promises an exhilarating and cerebral experience in "Caloop," inviting players to spin their way through a world where mathematical prowess meets strategic gameplay.
