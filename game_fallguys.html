Dive into fierce competition in Fall Guys. Race, jostle, and outwit rivals in a whimsical battle royale. Only the quickest, cleverest contestant emerges victorious in this hilarious and unpredictable challenge.Fall Guys is raucous, ridiculous fun. Some players will be reminded of 1970s family gameshow It’s a Knockout, while others will see a resemblance to Takeshi’s Castle: both involved the ritual humiliation of participants through zany tasks and both featured a compelling mix of knockabout comedy and sociopathic competitiveness. Fall Guys is the perfect heir to those legacies for the broadband generation, a game made for Twitch and YouTube streaming.
